Our Story

Founded by Siddharth Rasayali, osmowala_ began with a simple passion — capturing life’s moments in the most cinematic way possible. Starting from Vadodara with just a camera and a vision, we set out to turn everyday stories into timeless films. What makes us different is our focus on slow, smooth, and emotional visuals rather than fast transitions. Every frame we create is designed to connect with people — to make them feel the story, not just see it. From weddings to brand films, product shoots to cultural festivals, our journey has been built on creativity, trust, and consistency. With over 35+ projects and 30+ happy clients, osmowala_ continues to grow as a name people can count on for cinematic storytelling. For us, it’s not just about shooting videos — it’s about crafting experiences that stay with you forever.
